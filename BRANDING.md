# Caelum Brand

Visual identity for Caelum, the open source AMM on Soroban.

## Wordmark

Caelum, set in a serif display face. Latin for "sky". Always one word.

## Colors

| token | hex | use |
|---|---|---|
| Stellar Blue | `#4aa3df` | primary accent |
| Deep Sky | `#1e4a72` | secondary surface |
| Ice White | `#eaf6ff` | text on dark, focal area |
| Navy Void | `#0a1f3d` | background |
| Cyan Edge | `#88c0e8` | hover, captions |

## Logo files

- `assets/caelum-logo.svg` — square mark with bonding curve, use for avatars
- `assets/caelum-banner.svg` — wide banner with wordmark and AMM curves

## Voice

Mathematical, precise, dry. We explain the protocol, not the trade. No emoji, no hype.

## Don't

- do not invert the colour scheme on light backgrounds
- do not animate the bonding curve in the wordmark
- do not use the wordmark next to other AMM logos (Uniswap, Curve, etc.) without comparable spacing
